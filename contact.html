Before You Contact Us

Here are answers to the most common questions we receive:

Download not working?

Make sure you're using a valid UpScrolled share link that starts with https://share.upscrolled.com/. Private videos cannot be downloaded.

Video quality issues?

We download videos at the quality they're available on UpScrolled. If the original video is low quality, the download will reflect that.

Can't find your download?

Check your device's Downloads folder. On mobile devices, you may need to check the Files app (iPhone) or Downloads/Gallery (Android).
