Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Here's how we protect it.

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Overview

UpScrolled Video Downloader ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our Service.

The short version: We don't collect, store, or share any personal information. We don't track you. We don't store the videos you download.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we don't do:

  • We do not store videos: Videos are processed in real-time and never saved on our servers
  • We do not store links: The URLs you paste are used only to process your download and are immediately discarded
  • We do not require accounts: No registration, no login, no personal information needed
  • We do not track browsing: No tracking pixels, no analytics that identify you
  • We do not create profiles: We don't build profiles about our users
  • We do not sell data: We have no data to sell

Information That May Be Collected Automatically

Like most websites, our servers may automatically collect certain technical information:

  • IP Address: Your IP address may be temporarily logged for security and abuse prevention purposes. These logs are automatically deleted within 24 hours.
  • Request Data: Basic request information (timestamp, request type) may be logged for server operation purposes.

This information is not used to identify individuals and is not shared with any third parties.

Cookies

We use only essential cookies that are strictly necessary for the website to function. These cookies:

  • Do not track you across websites
  • Do not contain personal information
  • Are not used for advertising
  • Are session-based and expire when you close your browser

We do not use:

  • Third-party cookies
  • Analytics cookies
  • Advertising cookies
  • Social media tracking cookies

Third-Party Services

Our Service does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not:

  • Use Google Analytics or similar services
  • Display advertisements
  • Use social media tracking pixels
  • Share data with any third parties

Data Processing

When you use our Service to download a video:

  1. You provide an UpScrolled share link
  2. Our server fetches the video stream in real-time
  3. The video is converted to MP4 format
  4. The file is streamed directly to your device
  5. All temporary files are immediately deleted

No video content is retained on our servers after your download is complete.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access or alteration of data. Our security practices include:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Minimal data collection (we can't lose what we don't have)
  • Automatic deletion of temporary files

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided information to us, please contact us immediately.

International Users

Our Service may be accessed from various countries. By using the Service, you acknowledge that any data processing occurs in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal information, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. However, if you have any concerns about privacy, you have the right to:

  • Contact us with questions about our privacy practices
  • Request information about what data, if any, we may have
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority if you believe your rights have been violated

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
