# UpScrolled Video Downloader

A free online tool to download videos from UpScrolled. No login required, no watermarks, fast and easy.

## Features

- **Free to Use** - Download unlimited videos at no cost
- **No Watermark** - Clean downloads without any branding
- **No Login Required** - Just paste the link and download
- **Fast Processing** - Videos are processed quickly
- **Multiple Quality Options** - Download in available quality
- **Mobile Friendly** - Works on all devices

## How It Works

1. Open UpScrolled app and find the video you want to download
2. Tap "Share" and copy the video link
3. Paste the link on our website
4. Click "Download" and save the video
