How to Download UpScrolled Videos

Follow these simple steps to save any UpScrolled video to your device

  1. Open UpScrolled and Find Your Video

    Launch the UpScrolled app on your phone and browse to the video you want to download. You can find videos in your feed, search for specific content, or go to a creator's profile.

  2. Tap Share and Copy the Link

    Look for the Share button on the video (usually an arrow icon). Tap it and select "Copy Link" from the sharing options. The UpScrolled share link will be copied to your clipboard.

  3. Paste the Link on Our Website

    Open your web browser and go to UpScrolled Video Downloader. Tap or click on the input field and paste the link you just copied. The link should look like: https://share.upscrolled.com/en/post/...

  4. Click Download and Save Your Video

    Click the "Download Video" button and wait while we process your request. This usually takes 10-30 seconds depending on the video length. Once ready, click "Download MP4" to save the video to your device.

Tips for Downloading UpScrolled Videos

  • Check the link format: Make sure the link starts with https://share.upscrolled.com/
  • Public videos only: Private or restricted videos cannot be downloaded
  • Be patient: Longer videos may take more time to process
  • Use a stable connection: A good internet connection ensures faster downloads
  • Clear your clipboard: Copy the new link fresh if you're having issues

Download Process FAQ

When viewing a video, look for the share icon (usually an arrow pointing up or to the right). Tap it, and you'll see options to share. Select "Copy Link" to get the URL.

Download time depends on the video length and quality. Short videos typically process in 10-15 seconds, while longer videos may take up to a minute. If it takes longer than 2 minutes, try refreshing and pasting the link again.

On most devices, downloaded videos are saved to your Downloads folder. On iPhone, videos may go to the Files app. On Android, check your Downloads folder or Gallery app.
