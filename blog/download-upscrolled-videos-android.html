Device-Specific Tips

Samsung Galaxy

Use Samsung Internet or Chrome. Downloads go to your Downloads folder. The video will appear in Samsung Gallery after a few moments. If it doesn't show up immediately, open Gallery and tap the refresh option or "Show More" in the Albums section.

Google Pixel

Chrome works perfectly on Pixel phones. Videos download to the Downloads folder and appear in Google Photos automatically. You can also find them in the Files app.

OnePlus, Xiaomi, Oppo & Others

Use Chrome or your default browser. Downloads save to the standard Downloads folder. Check your Files app or Gallery to access the videos.

Troubleshooting Common Issues

Download not starting?

Make sure your browser has permission to download files. Go to Settings > Apps > Chrome > Permissions and ensure Storage is enabled.

Video not found error?

Double-check that the video is still available on UpScrolled and that it's set to public. Private videos cannot be accessed.

Video won't play after download?

Try opening the video with a different player app like VLC or MX Player. The default video player should work, but third-party players handle more formats.

Not enough storage?

Videos can be large, especially in HD quality. Make sure you have enough free space before downloading. You can check your storage in Settings > Storage.
