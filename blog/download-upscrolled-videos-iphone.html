What You'll Need

  • An iPhone or iPad running iOS 13 or later
  • Safari browser (recommended for best results)
  • The UpScrolled app with the video you want to download
  • A stable internet connection

Step 1: Find the Video in UpScrolled

Open the UpScrolled app on your iPhone and navigate to the video you want to download. You can find videos in your feed, through search, or on a creator's profile page.

Make sure the video is publicly accessible. Private or restricted videos cannot be downloaded.

Step 2: Copy the Share Link

Once you've found the video:

  1. Tap the Share button (usually an arrow icon)
  2. Select "Copy Link" from the sharing options
  3. The link is now copied to your clipboard

The share link typically looks like: https://share.upscrolled.com/en/post/...
